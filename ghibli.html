MIYAZAKI-ESQUE






Miyazaki is best known for his work at Studio Ghibli his artwork has been said to evoke nostalgia and a sense of calm with individuals all collectively stating that his art is magically immersive realism.

In order to tell a compelling and effective story, storytellers must construct a magically immersive world in which the story takes place; a process known as worldbuilding. Some stories require more detailed worldbuilding than others, but in the end the point is the same: suspend our disbelief, draw us in, make us buy into the world that has been created, and Miyazaki has been known to do just that. Miyazaki uses worldbuilding to achieve a sense of immersive realism that strike a difficult balance between fantasy and reality.
