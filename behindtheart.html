Behind The Art

Lofi girl’s youtube channel originally known as chilled cow; was looking for an artist to draw a “student busy revising for her classes with Miyazki-esque visuals”.

Low fidelity music paired with study has been continually rising in popularity with many saying their is scientific evidence that lofi music helps studying. Chilled Cow decided to call artists to create imagery along with the sound. We can thank Chilled Cow's vision and Juan Pablo Machado's artistry for the creation of LOFI GIRL.
